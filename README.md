# 🚌 SmartBus AI – Real-Time Bus Tracking & Digital Ticketing Platform

SmartBus AI is a modern web application designed to improve the public transportation experience by providing real-time bus tracking, route information, digital ticket booking, QR code-based ticket generation, and journey management.

## 🌐 Live Demo

🔗 **Website:** [https://smart-bus-map--g22310608.replit.app/dashboard](https://smart-bus-map--g22310608.replit.app/)

---

## 📌 Project Overview

SmartBus AI helps passengers easily track buses, view complete routes, estimate arrival times, purchase digital tickets, and manage their journeys through an intuitive and responsive interface.

The platform combines real-time tracking, digital ticketing, payment workflow, and passenger information into a single smart transportation solution.

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Search for a bus by route or bus number.
2. Track the live location of the bus.
3. View the complete route and upcoming stops.
4. Select your pickup and destination stops.
5. The fare is calculated automatically.
6. Complete the payment.
7. Receive a QR code-based digital ticket.
8. Track your journey until you reach your destination.
